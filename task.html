Services Section

BookMyShow offers a wide range of services, primarily focused on online ticketing for entertainment events. These include movies, concerts, plays, sporting events, and more. Beyond ticketing, BookMyShow also provides services for event organizers, such as on-ground support, reporting, and custom ticket pricing. They also offer corporate booking services and have a dedicated support center for users

About Section

BookMyShow, owned by Bigtree Entertainment Pvt Ltd, is India's leading entertainment ticketing platform. Launched in 2007, it has grown from a movie ticketing service to a platform offering tickets for movies, events, sports, and plays across India. BookMyShow also provides turnkey ticketing solutions for events and generates revenue from ticket sales, advertising, and food and beverage sales
